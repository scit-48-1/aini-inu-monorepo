## Resolution

root_cause: |
  Three sources of FK constraints block post deletion:
  1. Comment.post (@ManyToOne + @JoinColumn) -> generates FK from comment.post_id to post.id (this is the constraint in the error)
  2. PostLike.post (@ManyToOne + @JoinColumn) -> generates FK from post_like.post_id to post.id (would fail next even if comments were handled)
  3. Post.imageUrls (@ElementCollection + @CollectionTable) -> generates FK from post_image_url.post_id to post.id (Hibernate likely handles this one automatically)

  The service layer (PostService.deletePost) does NOT delete comments or likes before deleting the post.
  All FK constraints are auto-generated by Hibernate from JPA annotations; none are defined in explicit DDL.
